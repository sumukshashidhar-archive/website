Our Vision

We've experienced traditional college consultants ourselves: we thus know they're not worth anyone's time or money. Who would you trust more - a counselor with no in-field knowledge of the admissions world, or a peer who only recently went through the process? Good advice-always free.

Features

Current Undergraduates

We are undergraduate students that have gone through the college admissions process, and thus know the ins and outs of the process. We learnt this through a variety of disorganized sources: Our vision now, is to offer you a centralized resource for all your admissions needs.

Standardised Testing

Tutoring is more expensive than ever before, with large companies charging upto $5000 for 20 hours of tutoring. Having scored in the 99th percentile in these tests ourselves without any external help, we have free, honest tips and tricks to help you achieve your desired score.

No Sugarcoating

College consulting companies tend to oversell some parts of the application that they have special services for - to ensure that you’re trapped in their web of spending money. We don’t do that, and give you the brutal honest truth and ways you can feasibly improve your application.

Diverse Backgrounds

We are international students who come from all walks of life. Our diversity allows us to provide relevant advice to students, who like us, hail from different backgrounds and have varying aspirations.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why Us?

    It's pretty simple. College consultancies charge outrageous amounts of money: ranging from a minimum of 1 lakh INR in India, to more than 5000 USD a year in the United States- with schemes in place to extract even more money. We don’t do that. We’re just students giving you honest advice for free on our YouTube channel and blog. We did the research ourself in our time, scouring the internet and talking to students, and we want to make the process easier for you!

  • True, but College Consultancies are more reliable right?

    False! We, seconded by countless others, have seen that many college consultancies are involved in the fabrication of achievements and extracurriculars to make your application better, which has serious implications in the future. Many also “buy” success stories from candidates, and are run by people who have had absolutely no contact with the admissions world for a long time, just to make money. We are students who are recent undergraduates at top universities for our majors who were appalled by the situation and lack of resources and wanted to make it right.

  • Why can’t I do it myself? What is your angle here?

    You CAN do it yourself. That is what we want to help you with. All our content will be free, forever on our youtube channel and blog, and you can extract all the value you want without paying a DIME. We only ask you to share it with others if you find it helpful. We make most of our revenue through ads, only enough to keep us running, and to keep you bringing new and better content. If you have particularly unique situation, and/or want to get personalised feedback or guidance from one of our team members, we charge a nominal fee, as we cannot service all requests. However, we will only take your case and charge you if we feel that our videos cannot help you with your situation, else, we’ll just redirect you to the appropriate resources without charging you anything.

  • This is all great, but studying in the abroad is only for the rich, and I'm just a middle-class kid

    Incredibly false! Most of our team has either won hefty scholarships and full-rides (a term which indicates that the university takes care of every single expense you could have), or is taking out easily-paid-back loans to fund their education. This is a wrong notion that is brought about by college counsellors to scare you into either not pursuing your dream, or paying them money to help you get into a cheaper university elsewhere, earning a quick commission off of your admission.
